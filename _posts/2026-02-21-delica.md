The Mitsubishi Delica is one of the most popular (if not _the_ most popular) light trucks in Taiwan. The iconic L300 variant (aka the [Third Generation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mitsubishi_Delica#Third_generation_(1986)))[^1] has been manufactured here by China Motor Corporation (CMC), Mitsubishi’s local manufacturing partner, and is ubiquitous on the island. Even though the L300 has been discontinued in Japan in 1998, CMC has continued producing and modernising versions of the same design in Taiwan, and it is still possible to [purchase it new](https://www.mitsubishi-motors.com.tw/delicapu)[^2].

I’ve started noticing the Delicas on our last trip, but this time around, hunting for them has become a bit of an obsession: I’ve been collecting different variants and liveries (my favourite is the very colourful school bus version!), which I’m sharing below. 

This might yet turn into a proper project with time, but for now, enjoy the photos!
