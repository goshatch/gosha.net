About

Hi! My name is Gueorgui1 and I am a Russian-born, French-raised photographer, designer, and programmer living and working in London.

I have been online since the mid-1990's, and have been working with the Web ever since then. I studied software engineering at Epitech in Paris in the early 2000s, and more recently photography at the Royal College of Art in London.

Many of my interests lie at the intersection of art and technology, and this website is intended as an online space for documenting and archiving various projects I'm involved with.

For the last 2 years, I've been running A Possible Space, a tiny creative studio I've co-founded with my wife. Before that, among other things, I've been the CTO of product design agency AQ in Tokyo, and co-founded the civic tech startup Local Republic.

I welcome all kinds of collaborations!
