Hello! I'm Gueorgui Tcherednitchenko, a Russian-born, French-raised photographer, designer, and developer, living and working in London, UK.

The above images are from the project “Where The Story Begins” (2018–Ongoing), which premiered at the Royal College of Art Show 2019.

The exhibition is accompanied by a book, published by A Possible Space.
