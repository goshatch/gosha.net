In an effort to get back in programming shape, I'm making time to deepen my studies of Ruby (because I work with it daily) and Lisp (because I can use a variant of it, Emacs Lisp, to basically live inside my text editor)

Rethinking my workflows

I've moved from mac OS to GNU/Linux as my primary operating system a couple of months ago, and I'm now in the process of rebuilding my workflows on this new platform.

I chose Linux because I wanted to build my work environment around free, open source software, and I'm having a blast figuring out new and powerful new ways to do my work.
