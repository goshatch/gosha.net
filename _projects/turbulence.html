My first zine, containing work from 2015-2016. An intensely personal documentary of changes both private and public, set in Tokyo, Japan.

44 pages of black and white photographs, taken in 2015 and 2016.
Uncoated recycled paper, soft-cover, wire-stitched.
Edition of 100, self-published, sold out.
